A collection of data analysis projects from my early career, demonstrating skills in SQL querying, Python scripting, and business intelligence reporting.

## Projects included

- **Sales analysis** — SQL-based deep dive into sales trends, cohort analysis, and revenue attribution
- **HR analytics** — Python + Pandas exploration of employee retention patterns and headcount modeling
- **Financial reporting** — Power BI dashboards for budget vs actuals tracking
- **Customer segmentation** — k-means clustering in Python to segment customers by purchase behaviour

## Skills demonstrated

- Writing complex multi-join SQL queries against relational databases
- Data cleaning and transformation with Pandas
- Building interactive Power BI reports with DAX measures
- Statistical analysis and visualization with matplotlib / seaborn

## Context

These projects were built during my time at BlackRock and early Ericsson, and represent the foundation that led to the more complex production tools I build today.
